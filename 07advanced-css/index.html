Beaches

Finding the Best Wave

Professional surfers spend their whole life searching for the perfect wave to surf. Details like consistency, channels, panels, and swell are important when picking a beach to surf at.--SOURCE

Popular areas in the world known for surfing:

  • Gold Coast, Australia
  • Bali, Indonesia
  • Oahu, Hawaii
  • Santa Cruz, California
  • Puerto Escondido, Mexioc

What Makes a Good Beach?

Below are a few terms that are considered when picking a good beach to surf at.

Consistency
Watching live videos and reports to ensure consistent waves
Swell
Energy that has been transferred into the sea by wind
Channel
A current that usually runs perpendicular to the beach and provides a quick and safe route into the surf zone

Where Can I Go?

Besides beginner-friendly waves, there are other aspects that you should consider when planning your first surfing trip. Good vibes, highly rated surf camps with experienced instructors, the crowd factor, localism, the food, and the nightlife are just as important.
